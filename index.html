Painting a Miniature

Step by step guide to painting a 28mm scale miniature

Step 1: Models come out of the box connected to plastic supports called "sprues." Make a note of which pieces are for each model


Step 2: Take a pair of snippers and cut the connecting plastic between sprue and piece


Step 3: Glue pieces together in appropriate position


Step 4: Prime pieces with a flat paint color from a spray can (typically white, black, or grey)


Step 5: Base coat model with primary color that will be used on finished product. This step is often done with a spray can but can be done with a brush for a virtually identical product


Step 6: Paint fine details with paint brush. For this step it is smart to use a reference from a completed model to know which colors are appropriate.

This step is much more complicated than described above. It is wise to watch a tutorial on how to paint such a model. Tutorial.
